COMPETITION RULES

I. FORMAT & OVERVIEW

  • 100 contestants will participate in 3 main rounds similar to the "Golden Bell" format.
  • Contestants answer by showing their boards within the time limit.
  • Official answers must match the program's. The Organizer's decision is final in all situations.

II. VIOLATIONS

  • Cheating, using phones, or bringing A/V devices will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Contestants are not allowed to discuss on the stage.
  • If a class's supporter prompts the answer, their contestant will be disqualified immediately.

III. ROUNDS OVERVIEW

  • Round 1 (Easy): 15s/question. Incorrect answers lead to temporary elimination. Plays until < 40 left.
  • Round 2 (Medium): 20s/question. Incorrect answers lead to temporary elimination. Plays until 5 left.
  • Round 3 (Hard): 30s/question. Incorrect answers lead to elimination. Plays until the winner is found.
  • Rescue Round: Organized if too many are eliminated. A minigame helps select exactly 5 contestants to return.

IV. SUPPORTER GUIDELINES

  • Maintain order during the competition. Do NOT reveal answers.
  • Prompting will lead to immediate disqualification of your class's contestant.
